## What is strong
- Comprehensive structure: baseline context, regulation, options, benchmarks, stakeholders, implementation roadmap.
- Correctly identifies key New Haven data gaps (inventory, throughput, outfalls, receiving-water monitoring).
- Strong emphasis on CT permit mechanics and operational controls.

## Risks / weaknesses
- Mixed source quality (primary regulatory docs mixed with news, vendor pages, and corporate self-reporting).
- Several quantitative claims are not yet independently verified from primary records (especially volume/throughput claims).
- Some benchmark projects are not apples-to-apples on scope, year, and cost basis.
- Citation integrity is partial: some citation numbers in the text have unresolved URLs after import.
